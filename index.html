Hi i'm Krishna,

i do urban sketching!

i wanted to share my art experiences!

Urban Sketching is my favourite medium of sketching now. The essence of an urban sketch is that its primary function is not to be beautiful, but to capture what it feels like to be human. This could be in a man-made building, the hand of man on the landscape, or human beings in action, wherever they are or whatever they are doing.
